Quantum Machine Learning

Quantum machine learning is a cutting edge field, with an as of yet undecided foundations. Currently implementable and effective models tend to utilize hybrid schemes, wherein classical machine learning techniques interact with quantum submodules to effectively get the best of both worlds.
